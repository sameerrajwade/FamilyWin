# FamilyWin — Frequently Asked Questions

A consumer-facing FAQ. Suitable for linking from the Play Store listing,
support emails, or the in-app help section.

## General

**Q: How much does FamilyWin cost?**
A: FamilyWin is free to download and use.

**Q: How many people can be in a family?**
A: There's no hard limit — add as many parents and children as your household needs.

**Q: Does everyone need their own phone?**
A: No. Younger children can have a "managed profile" — a parent marks
completions on their behalf, and the child can use **Child Mode** on a
shared or parent device.

**Q: What happens at the end of the week?**
A: Every week (configurable start day, default Sunday midnight), FamilyWin
automatically tallies points, ranks everyone, announces a winner, sends a
notification to the whole family, and starts a fresh week — all hands-free.

## Points & Tasks

**Q: How are points decided?**
A: Each task is rated Easy (10 pts), Medium (25 pts), or Hard (50 pts) when
it's created. Parents can also log one-off bonuses or penalties for things
that aren't regular chores.

**Q: What is "auto-fail"?**
A: If a task has a deadline ("auto-fail hour") and nobody marks it complete
in time, FamilyWin automatically deducts the points — no parent reminder
required.

**Q: I tapped a task by accident — can I undo it?**
A: Yes — tap the same task again on the **same day** and choose "Undo". This
reverses both the completion and the points it awarded. Undo isn't available
after the day ends (it becomes part of the permanent history).

**Q: What's a streak, and how do I get one?**
A: Completing your tasks on consecutive days builds a streak. Hit 7, 14, or
30 days in a row and you automatically earn a bonus (+50 / +100 / +250 points).

## Rewards

**Q: How do I redeem points?**
A: Go to the Rewards tab, pick something your family has set up, and tap
"Redeem". A parent will see your request and approve or reject it — points
are only spent once approved.

**Q: Why didn't my points go down right when I requested a reward?**
A: By design — points are only deducted when a parent approves the request,
so you don't lose points on a request that gets declined.

## Profiles & Photos

**Q: How do I add a profile photo?**
A: Open your profile (tap your row in the Family tab) → "Edit Profile" →
choose a photo from your camera or photo library. If you don't set a photo,
your chosen emoji avatar is shown instead — and that's totally fine!

**Q: I tapped the photo picker and nothing happened — what do I do?**
A: Give it a second — on some devices, the picker can take a moment to
appear the very first time. Avoid tapping repeatedly; one tap is enough.
If it still doesn't appear, close and reopen the app.

## Notifications

**Q: I'm not getting reminders — what should I check?**
A: 1) Confirm notifications are enabled for FamilyWin in your phone's system
settings, 2) check your reminder time in the app's Settings tab, and 3) on
newer Android phones, make sure FamilyWin is **excluded from battery
optimization** — phones aggressively limit background apps by default, which
can silently block reminders and the auto-fail check.

## Privacy & Data

**Q: Can other families see our data?**
A: No. Every family's data is completely walled off — enforced at the
database level, not just hidden in the app. Nothing is ever shared, compared,
or sold across families.

**Q: How do I delete my account or family's data?**
A: Contact support (see the listing's support email) and we'll process a
full deletion. (If you're the developer reading this pre-launch: build and
document an in-app deletion flow before public release — Play Store policy
increasingly expects one.)

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Something seems "stuck" — the leaderboard isn't updating, a button does
nothing, etc.**
A: FamilyWin updates in real time over the internet — first check your
connection, then close and reopen the app. Most sync hiccups resolve within
a few seconds. If a problem persists, contact support with a description of
what you tapped and what you expected to happen.
