Part 1 — Installing FamilyWin

Step 1: Download the app

Get FamilyWin from the Google Play Store on your Android phone. Search "FamilyWin" or follow the link your family admin shares with you.

See the full Screen Gallery for visual mockups of every screen in the app.

Step 2: Create your account

Open the app and choose Sign Up. You can register with an email + password, or tap Continue with Google for a one-tap sign-in.

See the full Screen Gallery for visual mockups of every screen in the app.

Step 3: Create or join a family

The first person sets up the family — give it a name and you'll get a unique 6-character invite code. Everyone else taps Join a Family and enters that code.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Part 2 — Setting things up (for parents)

Step 4: Add family members

Go to the Family tab → Add Child (or invite a partner with the family code). For young kids without their own phone, create a managed profile — you'll be able to mark completions on their behalf.

Tap any member's row to open Edit Profile — change their name, age, avatar emoji, or upload a real photo. If a photo is set, it shows everywhere that member appears (rows, leaderboard, rewards, etc.); otherwise their emoji avatar is used.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Step 5: Create tasks

Go to Home → + Create Task. Give it a title, a category, and a difficulty:

DifficultyPoints Awarded
Easy10
Medium25
Hard50

Optionally set a recurrence (daily/weekly) and an auto-fail hour — if the task isn't completed by that time, points are automatically deducted, no reminders needed from you.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Step 6: Set up the rewards store

Go to Rewards → add items with a point cost (e.g. "30 minutes extra screen time — 100 points"). Kids can request to redeem; you approve or reject — points are only deducted once you approve.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Part 3 — Using the app every day

Step 7: Complete tasks & earn points

On the Home tab, tap a task card to mark it complete. You'll see a celebration animation and your points update instantly — for everyone in the family, in real time.

Made a mistake? Tap the same task again the same day to undo the completion (and reverse the points). Undo is only available until midnight.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Step 8: Check the leaderboard

The Leaderboard tab shows live rankings with a podium for the top 3, everyone's streaks, and how close the race is — updated the instant anyone completes (or undoes) a task.

View this screen in the Gallery →

Step 9: Track history & trends

The History tab shows a 6-week chart of your points and a full transaction log — every gain, loss, bonus, and redemption, permanently recorded.

See the full Screen Gallery for visual mockups of every screen in the app.

Step 10: Personalize your experience

In Settings, switch between Light, Dark, or Child Mode (bigger buttons, a friendly mascot, simpler language — great for younger kids), and configure notification times.

See the full Screen Gallery for visual mockups of every screen in the app.

Part 4 — Each Sunday at midnight

The week automatically resets: final scores are tallied, a winner is announced, everyone gets a notification, and a brand-new week begins — completely hands-free.

Need help?

Common questions and troubleshooting tips live in the project's GitHub repository. If something looks wrong (a write that "does nothing," a missing photo, etc.), try closing and reopening the app first — most sync hiccups resolve within a few seconds thanks to FamilyWin's real-time design.
