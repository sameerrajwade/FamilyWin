What is FamilyWin?

FamilyWin is a mobile app that replaces nagging and sticker charts with a fun, fair, automated chore-tracking game. Every family member — parents and kids alike — completes tasks, earns points in real time, and competes on a live leaderboard. The person with the most points when the week ends wins. Simple to understand, hard to put down.

How it works

  1. Create your family in under a minute and invite everyone with a simple 6-character code.
  2. Set up tasks — chores, homework, habits — each worth Easy / Medium / Hard points.
  3. Everyone completes tasks right from their phone, watching their points (and the leaderboard) update instantly.
  4. Every Sunday at midnight, the week resets — a winner is crowned, everyone's notified, and a new week begins.

Privacy first

Your family's data belongs to your family — full stop. FamilyWin never shares, compares, or mixes data across families. Everything is scoped and protected at the database level, not just hidden in the app.
